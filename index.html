Hello! I'm Katie, a part-time education researcher.

I'm currently exploring a few more options beyond education, looking for work that both feels like I do good and that actually does good. While those sound like similar goals that are a bit naive, I think they end up being surprisingly different and important to me.

I began my higher education believing I would go into astrophysics, but transitioned from the physical sciences to secondary education in my senior year of college. I've since taught, worked at a few nonprofits, and run summer programs. A generalist at heart, I have studied linguistics, earth sciences, astronomy, literature, biology, and behavioral sciences; that said, my enduring passions have been & continue to be learning about learning and helping people become their best selves.

This site (perhaps obviously) is very much a work-in-progress, so please check back later! I have quite a number of todos...

Laughter and tears are both responses to frustration and exhaustion. I myself prefer to laugh, since there is less cleaning do to do afterward.
